CartesianOperators.advection_diffusion! — Method
advection_diffusion!(out, adops, uω, uγ, Tω, Tγ, ...; scheme=Centered())

Compute κ*Δ(Tω,Tγ) + convection(uω,uγ,Tω,Tγ) in the sign convention used by laplacian! and convection!.

CartesianOperators.build_convection_parts — Method
build_convection_parts(cops, uω, uγ, Tω, Tγ)

Build per-dimension centered advection contributions (bulk and coupling) in assembled form. This uses periodic wrapping when requested by cops.bc_adv; otherwise stencil rows are inactive at the padded boundary.
